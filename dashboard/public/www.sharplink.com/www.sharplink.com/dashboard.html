ETH CONCENTRATION

ETH CONCENTRATION
ETH Concentration is calculated by dividing (A) the number of ETH and the as-if redeemed ETH Equivalents (as of midnight UTC on the most recent Sunday), that the Company holds by (B) each 1,000 Assumed Diluted Shares Outstanding. “Assumed Diluted Shares Outstanding” represents the sum of (i) the Company's actual shares of Common Stock issued and outstanding as of the end of each week, inclusive of equity raises, plus (ii) the additional shares that would be issued upon the assumed exercise or settlement of all Common Stock Equivalents, updated on a monthly basis. See our recent press releases and the Company's SEC filings for important additional information regarding how ETH Concentration and Assumed Diluted Shares Outstanding are calculated. ETH Concentration may experience temporary declines due to timing gaps between share issuance and ETH deployment and issuances of equity awards.

STAKING REWARDS

STAKING REWARDS
The total number of ETH, including as-if redeemed ETH Equivalent rewards (as of midnight UTC on the most recent Sunday), earned by the Company in staking rewards since inception of the Company’s treasury strategy. Under U.S. GAAP, native staking rewards are reported as revenue on the Company's financial statements. ETH Equivalent rewards are recognized in the financial statements as revenue from contracts with non customers when realized upon redemption. Certain aspects of our staking activities may be subject to government rules and regulation and, therefore, amounts are subject to change.

ETH NAV

ETH NAV
ETH NAV represents the total number of ETH and as-if redeemed ETH equivalents, the Company holds (as of midnight UTC on the most recent Sunday), updated on a weekly basis, multiplied by the current market price of one ETH (or the price of one ETH as of the date indicated). It does not take into account or include the Company's cash, indebtedness or the value or number of outstanding shares of its Common Stock and equivalents. As such, it is not equivalent to "net asset value" or "NAV" or any similar metric in the traditional financial context or to the NAV used by other companies; and it is not a measure of either the net asset value of the Company or the value of the ETH held by the Company, net of indebtedness and other obligations.

ETH GAIN (LOSS)

ETH GAIN (LOSS)
ETH GAIN (LOSS) is calculated as (A) the current price of ETH times the Company's ETH holdings, on an as-if-redeemed basis for any ETH equivalents (as of midnight UTC on the most recent Sunday), less (B) the total proceeds deployed to purchase ETH and ETH equivalents. It does not represent a realized or unrealized gain or loss as calculated using U.S. GAAP and reported on the financial statements.

BASIC MNAV

BASIC MNAV
Basic mNAV, or just "mNAV," represents a multiple of ETH NAV, as of the specified date, calculated as the Company's Enterprise Value divided by ETH NAV. Enterprise Value is calculated as (A) Market Cap, plus (B) Debt, less (C) the Company's weekly cash balance. See "Enterprise Value" for the full definition. As with ETH NAV, although mNAV incorporates the label "NAV," it is not equivalent to "net asset value" or "NAV" or any similar metric in the traditional financial context. Additionally, it is not a measure of the amount by which the Enterprise Value exceeds net asset value in the traditional financial sense of those terms. Investors should rely on the financial statements and other disclosures contained in the Company's SEC filings. This metric is a supplement, not a substitute, to the financial statements and other disclosures contained in the Company's SEC filings. It should be used only by sophisticated investors who understand its limited purpose and inherent limitations.
--

FULLY DILUTED MNAV

FULLY DILUTED MNAV
Fully Diluted mNAV is calculated as the Company's Fully Diluted Enterprise Value divided by ETH NAV. Fully Diluted Enterprise Value is the sum of (A) Fully Diluted Market Cap, calculated as Assumed Diluted Shares Outstanding times the closing price of the Company's Common Stock on The Nasdaq Capital Market on the applicable date, plus (B) Debt, less (C) the Company's weekly cash balance. This metric does not consider cash proceeds from warrant exercises. "Assumed Diluted Shares Outstanding" represents the sum of (i) the Company's actual shares of Common Stock issued and outstanding as of the end of each week, inclusive of equity raises, plus (ii) the additional shares that would be issued upon the assumed exercise or settlement of all Common Stock Equivalents, updated on a monthly basis.
--

ENTERPRISE VALUE

ENTERPRISE VALUE
Enterprise Value is calculated as (A) the number of issued and outstanding shares of Common Stock and outstanding Common Stock Equivalents (“Common Stock Equivalents” consist of outstanding warrants, pre-funded warrants, stock option awards and restricted stock units) times the closing price of the Company's Common Stock on The Nasdaq Capital Market on the applicable date, plus (B) Debt (the aggregate principal amount of the Company's outstanding indebtedness), less (C) the Company's weekly cash balance.
